The Search & Source Strategy

Where to Buy

  • • CPO (Certified Pre-Owned): The "Sweet Spot." You get the manufacturer warranty without the 20% "off-the-lot" depreciation.
  • • Private Sellers: Best raw value, but requires a pre-purchase inspection (PPI). Zero dealer markup or hidden fees.
  • • New Inventory: Only strategic if manufacturer subvented rates (0.9% - 2.9% APR) are available to preserve cash.

How to Pay

  • • Cash: Eliminates interest but creates a "Liquidity Drain." Recommended only if your Emergency Fund stays at 6+ months.
  • • Finance: Leverage the bank's capital to keep yours invested—if and only if the APR is lower than your portfolio yield.
2

Leasing vs. Owning

Leasing (Subscription)

Lifestyle Optimization

  • ✓ Cash Flow: Lower monthly payments than traditional financing.
  • ✓ Write-offs: Direct expense deduction for business owners.
  • ✕ Wealth Trap: You pay for the steepest part of depreciation (years 1-3) forever.

Owning (Asset Build)

Wealth Optimization

  • ✓ Equity Window: The "Magic" happens in years 5–10 with $0 monthly payments.
  • ✓ Residual Value: You retain an asset that can be liquidated or traded.
  • ✕ Initial Load: Requires higher down payments and monthly obligations.
ThryvIQ Verdict

If keeping the vehicle for 6+ years, buying wins 100% of the time. Lease only if your business tax savings exceed the depreciation cost.
